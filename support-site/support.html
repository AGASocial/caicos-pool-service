Frequently asked questions

How do I get an account?

Cadenza accounts are created by your company's owner or admin in the Cadenza web portal. If you're a technician, ask your manager to send you an invite link.

I didn't receive my invite link.

Invite links expire after 7 days. Ask your admin to resend it, or email us and we'll help.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. Cadenza Crew works offline at job sites and syncs automatically once you're back online.

How do I reset my password?

Use "Forgot password?" on the sign-in screen, or contact your admin.

How do I delete my account or data?

Email us at byagasocial@gmail.com and we'll process your request.
