What we’re building

Everything your team needs.
Nothing they don’t.

Built for the realities of pool service — sun, water, and tight schedules.

Digital service reports

Every visit becomes a clear report — photos, issue flags, and notes — in one fast flow. No paper, no guesswork.

Smart route scheduling

Plan daily routes for each technician, set stop order, and track progress in real time from the admin portal.

Photo documentation

Proof-of-visit photos with GPS and timestamp sync to the office instantly. Protect your business and keep customers informed.

Team management

Invite technicians by email, assign roles, and manage your crew from a central admin dashboard. No IT required.

Property database

Store gate codes, pool specs, customer contacts, and notes for every property. Technicians see exactly what they need on-site.

Reports & exports

Search, filter, and export service reports as PDF or CSV. Share with customers or use for billing — in seconds.

FAQ

Questions before you join.

Common questions from pool companies exploring early access.

Who is Cadenza for?

Pool service companies of any size — from solo operators to crews with dozens of technicians. If you run routes, document visits, and need your office and field teams in sync, Cadenza is built for you.

When will Cadenza launch publicly?

We’re in private beta now. Founding companies on the waitlist get early access, help shape the product, and lock in founding-member pricing when we launch.

Does the technician app work offline?

Yes. Technicians can complete visits, capture photos, and add notes without cell service. Everything syncs automatically when they’re back online.

What devices are supported?

The admin portal runs in any modern web browser. The technician app works on iOS and Android phones.

How much does it cost?

We haven’t published pricing yet. Early-access partners receive founding-member rates and a direct line to our team during beta.
