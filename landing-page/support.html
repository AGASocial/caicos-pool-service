Getting started

How do I get an account?

Cadenza accounts are created by your company's owner or admin in the Cadenza web portal. Owners can sign up and create their company directly; technicians and admins join by invitation. If you're a technician, ask your manager to send you an invite link.

I didn't receive my invite link

Invite links expire after 7 days. Ask your admin to resend the invite from the Team section of the portal. If it still doesn't arrive, check your spam folder or email us and we'll help.

How do I reset my password?

Use the "Forgot password?" link on the sign-in screen to receive a reset email, or ask your company admin to assist.

Using the app

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. The Cadenza technician app works offline at job sites and automatically syncs your photos, readings, and reports once you're back online — so nothing is ever lost.

How do I log a service?

Open today's route, tap the job you're working on, and complete the service report: capture photos, enter chemical readings and equipment checks, run the task checklist, and add any notes or follow-up flags. Submit when you're done and it syncs to your office automatically.

Why can't I see other companies' data?

Cadenza isolates every company's data at the database level. You'll only ever see information that belongs to your own company and your role.

Account & data

How do I delete my account or data?

Email us at support@cadenzaops.com and we'll process your request. Company owners can also remove team members directly from the portal.
