07 · Iconography · Photography · Motion
Iconography

Even, geometric, unfilled

Line icons, 1.5px stroke at 24px, geometric construction with lightly rounded joins (~2px). Outline-only in UI; filled reserved for status. Lucide / Phosphor (regular) as the reference set — matches the wordmark’s calm confidence.

Photography

Editorial, not stock

Real field craft shot like premium lifestyle — natural light, warm-neutral, lightly desaturated. Hands and tools mid-task: the performance, not staged smiles. Generous negative space for wordmark overlay. Avoid pool-cyan; stay vertical-agnostic.

Motion

Slow, eased, restrained

240–420ms on cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1). Never bouncy. Signature: the brass “echo” — the C or an underline reveals with one faint trailing echo on load. Page transitions: fade + 8px rise. Decoration earns its place or it’s cut.
